Privacy Policy

Last updated: 8 April 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Calibrated Productions Pty. Ltd. ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and stores information when you use Hopper and visit hopperplayer.com.

Information we collect

We collect the following information when you use Hopper or purchase a license:

  • Name and email address — provided at the time of purchase or when joining our waitlist.
  • Computer information — hardware model, CPU architecture, RAM, macOS version, and a hashed IP address collected when Hopper checks for updates. This information is used solely for analytics and compatibility planning.
  • License activation data — machine fingerprint used to enforce machine limits on your license. This is processed by our licensing provider, Keygen.sh.

How we store your data

We store your name, email address, and computer information in a private Cloudflare D1 database managed by Calibrated Productions Pty. Ltd. License activation and machine fingerprint data is additionally stored by Keygen.sh, our third-party licensing service. All data is held in accordance with Cloudflare's and Keygen.sh's respective data security standards.

Payments are processed by Stripe. We do not store credit card or payment details — these are handled entirely by Stripe.

Show archivals and diagnostic screenshots

If you choose to submit a diagnostic report (via Help › Send Diagnostics… in the app), it may include screenshots or excerpts from your show archival video to help us reproduce and resolve the reported issue. This content is:

  • Used exclusively for diagnosing and resolving the specific issue you reported.
  • Stored securely and accessible only to Calibrated Productions Pty. Ltd. staff.
  • Deleted within 6 months of the issue being resolved.
  • Never shared with third parties or used for any other purpose.

Diagnostic submissions are always voluntary. Hopper does not transmit any video content without your explicit action.

How we use your information

  • To issue and manage your Hopper license.
  • To send transactional emails (license keys, renewal notices, account changes).
  • To notify waitlist subscribers when Hopper becomes available for purchase.
  • To understand which macOS versions and hardware configurations to prioritise.
  • To diagnose issues you report to us.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Third-party services

  • Cloudflare — database and edge infrastructure.
  • Keygen.sh — software licensing.
  • Stripe — payment processing.
  • Resend — transactional email delivery.

Data retention

Purchase and license records are retained for the duration of your subscription and for a period of 7 years thereafter for financial record-keeping purposes. Update analytics (computer information) are retained for 2 years. Waitlist email addresses are deleted upon request or once Hopper is no longer in active development. Diagnostic content is deleted within 6 months of the associated issue being resolved.

Your rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information at any time by contacting us at hello@hopperplayer.com.
